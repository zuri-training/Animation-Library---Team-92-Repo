Basics

Account

I am having troubles logging in, how can I reset my password?

If you are yet to sign up (unauthorised user), then you will need to sign up to generate a password. If you have forgotten your password, please go to this link to request for password reset: https://xxxxxxxx/password/reset (You will need to enter your email address and a password reset link will be sent to your email.) Or click forgot password on the sign in page. If you are already signed in, but cannot remember your password, You can reset your password here under your Account Settings.

How can u edit and update my profile?

Click the user Icon on your dashboard to view your profile page/Account. And then click on “Edit Profile” to edit your profile.

Why can’t I change my email address?

You probably have not verified your email. To change your email address or your password, firstly you will need to make sure that you have verified your email address. Go to Edit profile on your account Scroll down to email, if you haven’t verified your email, you will see a red sign on your email asking you to verify. Click to verify.

How can i change my email address?

To Change Email Address; 1) Go to 'Edit Profile' on your account 2) Click 'Account Settings' 3) Enter your new email and click send verification. Resend if the email doesn't arrive. 4) You will get a confirmation email, go click that verification link in the email. 5) You can choose to set a new password after verifying the email.

What happens when I delete my account?

To delete your account; Go to Settings on your dashboard In Settings, click on Manage Account. Click Delete Account. Read through to understand what happens when you delete your account. Click delete account if you still want to go through with it. To proceed, confirm your action when asked on the in-app pop-up message. You will get a notification of deletion in progress, you can cancel deletion before the 24-hrs grace elapses.

How do I delete an account?

Go to Settings on your dashboard In Settings, click on Manage Account. Click Delete Account

GENERAL ANIMATION QUESTIONS

Why should I use Moov?

Moov has a pool of pre-made animations at the disposal of the users, for them to embed their codes either by import, embed, or package manager installation. Users can also download the Library, save and create personalized Libraries that are specific to their projects and also be able to download later. Moov also shows users, usage examples on how to use the library, and also provides an interactive interface to generate the right combination of codes to use. Moov also has its complete features on all mobile devices which means you can complete your work anywhere, anytime and even on- the- go.

What is Moov?

Moov is an animation Library platform that allows beginners (designers & developers) to perform basic web animation using normal language. It is a platform that users visit and can view basic information about it, view and interact with the documentation, sign up to gain full access to the Library.

Who can use Moov?

Moov lets you create vector animations, advertisements, multimedia content, immersive experiences, applications, games, and more, in a timeline-based authoring environment. It also offers native support for multiple outputs such as HTML5 Canvas and WebGL, and is extensible to support custom formats such as Snap.SVG. Animate also supports creation and publishing of Flash formats, and packaging applications. Hence, Moov Is meant for EVERYONE. Designers, developers, motion graphics artists, marketers, basically CREATIVES. Note that; Moov’s flexibility with output formats ensure that your content can be viewed anywhere, without the need for plug-ins.

Can I animate illustrations on Moov?

Anything can be animated on moov - words, letters, illustrations, images etc.

Can animate Logos on Moov?

Yes, you can.

Can I edit animation properties to suit my preference?

Yes, you can. Moov’s interface is interactive and hence allows you to interact with the codes, embed your own codes, and create your personalised animations.

What formats can I import files to be animated?

You can either import your html files to the platform and embed your codes on the platform, or download our library to be able to use our animations. For Images; png, jpeg, Ssvg
